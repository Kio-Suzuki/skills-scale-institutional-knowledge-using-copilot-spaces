# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers design user experiences, create prototypes, and ensure products meet customer needs. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to translate user requirements into intuitive, accessible interfaces.

### Responsibilities
- Design user experiences and create prototypes
- Gather and integrate user feedback into design iterations
- Collaborate with Product Managers to understand user needs and requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure designs are feasible and properly implemented
- Conduct usability testing and iterate based on findings
- Maintain design systems and ensure consistency across products

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences that meet customer needs
- Reduce friction in user workflows and improve satisfaction
- Ensure design decisions are validated through user research and testing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- Usability testing sessions and findings reports
- Handoff documentation for QA Engineers and Developers
- Collaboration in sprint planning to discuss design feasibility

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers own the quality process, ensuring products meet acceptance criteria and maintain high standards. They write and execute test plans, automate tests, and validate that releases are ready for customers.

### Responsibilities
- Write and execute comprehensive test plans
- Automate tests where possible to improve efficiency and coverage
- Validate that acceptance criteria are met before releases
- Sign off on releases and milestone readiness
- Collaborate with Developers on bug fixes and testing strategies
- Report and track defects through resolution

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability
- Prevent regressions through thorough testing
- Maintain high test coverage across all product areas

### Typical Communication
- Bug reports and test results shared with Developers
- Quality status updates to Project Managers for milestone readiness
- Acceptance criteria clarification with Product Managers
- Usability test coordination with UX Designers

---

## Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)

### Role Summary
Site Reliability Engineers ensure the reliability, uptime, and performance of systems. They contribute operational expertise during planning, implement monitoring and alerting, and participate in incident response to maintain system health.

### Responsibilities
- Ensure system reliability, uptime, and performance
- Contribute operational concerns and requirements during project planning
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting systems
- Participate in incident response and post-mortem analysis
- Build and maintain scalable infrastructure
- Optimize system performance and resource utilization

### Goals
- Maximize system availability and reliability
- Minimize Mean Time To Recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Build scalable, maintainable infrastructure

### Typical Communication
- Operational requirements during planning sessions with Developers and Project Managers
- Monitoring and alerting integration discussions with Developers
- System health check coordination with QA Engineers
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts translate business needs into technical requirements, ensuring that solutions align with organizational goals. They bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams, reducing ambiguity and validating outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Translate business needs into clear, actionable requirements
- Create process flows and documentation for business workflows
- Validate that solutions meet business goals and objectives
- Bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams
- Facilitate requirements gathering sessions
- Analyze and document current state vs. desired future state

### Goals
- Ensure technical solutions align with business objectives
- Reduce requirement ambiguity and miscommunication
- Improve efficiency of business processes through technology

### Typical Communication
- Requirements refinement sessions with Product Managers
- Scope validation meetings with Project Managers
- Stakeholder feedback sessions and validation reviews
- Business process documentation and flow diagrams

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers advocate for customer needs and ensure satisfaction throughout the product lifecycle. They collect feedback for continuous improvement, assist with onboarding, and surface post-launch issues to drive product excellence.

### Responsibilities
- Advocate for customer needs within the organization
- Surface post-launch issues and customer pain points
- Collect and synthesize customer feedback for product improvements
- Assist with customer onboarding and training
- Handle support escalations and critical customer issues
- Track customer satisfaction metrics and retention

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Provide actionable product feedback to drive improvements
- Ensure customers achieve their desired outcomes with the product

### Typical Communication
- Feature feedback and prioritization discussions with Product Managers
- Issue confirmation and reproduction with QA Engineers
- Customer-facing update scheduling with Project Managers
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
